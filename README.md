# 🌿 KeenKeeper - Meaningful Connection Manager

**KeenKeeper** is a modern relationship management dashboard designed to help you nurture and maintain your most important friendships. In a world of digital noise, KeenKeeper ensures you never lose touch with the people who matter most by tracking interactions and setting personal connection goals.

## ✨ Key Features

* **🎯 Intelligent Status Tracking:** Automatically categorizes your friends into `On-Track`, `Almost Due`, or `Overdue` based on your last interaction and personal frequency goals.
* **⚡ Quick Check-In System:** Log a call, text, or video chat with a single click. Every action is instantly recorded in a beautiful, interactive timeline.
* **📊 Personal Connection Stats:** Visualize your social health with summary cards showing total connections, those needing attention, and monthly interaction counts.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

KeenKeeper is built with a high-performance, modern stack:

- **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **UI Components:** [daisyUI](https://daisyui.com/)
- **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Notifications:** [React Hot Toast](https://react-hot-toast.com/)
- **Language:** JavaScript (JSX)
